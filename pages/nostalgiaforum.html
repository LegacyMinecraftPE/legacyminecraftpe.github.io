About

NostalgiaForum is a Minecraft PE Alpha forum created in 2023. The forum currently has over 500 topics and 202 resources. On the forum you can find most of the plugins from the no longer working PocketMine Forums, as well as new plugins from the community. Various maps, mods, textures, programs, clients, tutorials, etc.
